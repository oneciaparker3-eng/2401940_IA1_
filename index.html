AI Business Reflection

My Favourite AI Business Idea The AI business idea that interests me the most is AI for fraud detection and prevention in banking. Financial fraud is becoming more common as cybercriminals develop new ways to steal money and sensitive information. AI can help banks detect suspicious transactions quickly and accurately, making banking safer for customers.

How It Can Be Applied in My World

As a student pursuing a degree in Computer Networks and Security, I believe this technology is directly related to my future career. AI can monitor thousands of banking transactions in real time, identify unusual patterns, and alert security teams before major financial losses occur. In the future, I would like to contribute to developing or improving cybersecurity systems that protect financial institutions and their customers from cyber threats.

Ethical Considerations

One ethical concern is privacy. AI systems require access to large amounts of customer data to detect fraud, and this information must be handled responsibly. Another concern is that AI could mistakenly flag legitimate transactions as fraudulent, causing inconvenience to customers. There is also the risk of bias if AI systems are trained using incomplete or inaccurate data.

My Solution

To address these concerns, banks should implement strong data protection measures such as encryption and strict access controls to protect customer information. AI systems should also be regularly tested and updated to improve their accuracy. Finally, human security professionals should review high-risk transactions before final decisions are made, ensuring that customers are treated fairly.

Example of an App

Chapter 4 – Achieve Your Goals

Reading this chapter reminded me that I should set both short-term and long-term goals. My short-term goal is to successfully complete my degree in Computer Networks and Security, while my long-term goal is to become a cybersecurity professional. I learned that writing down my goals and reviewing them regularly will help me stay focused and overcome challenges.

Chapter 5 – The Proper Use of Time

This chapter teaches that time is one of our most valuable resources. Everyone has the same twenty-four hours each day, but successful people learn how to use their time wisely. The chapter encourages readers to prioritize important tasks instead of wasting time on activities that do not contribute to their goals.

This chapter made me reflect on how I manage my daily schedule. As a university student, I often balance assignments, studying, and personal responsibilities. I realize that improving my time management will reduce stress and help me complete my work more efficiently. By planning my day and avoiding procrastination, I can become more productive.

Chapter 6 – Surround Yourself with the Right People

The authors explain that the people we spend the most time with have a significant influence on our attitudes, habits, and success. Positive and supportive people encourage growth, while negative influences can prevent us from reaching our potential.

This chapter relates closely to my own life because I value the support of my family, friends, lecturers, and especially my partner. Chapter 7 – Be a Lifelong Learner

This chapter focuses on the importance of continuous learning and personal growth.
